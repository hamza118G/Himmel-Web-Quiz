Question Bank
In Question Mode, you can systematically go through all questions one by one. Each question comes with four answer options, and as you select and submit an answer, you'll receive instant feedback on whether your choice was correct or incorrect. After submission, a detailed explanation of the correct answer is provided. This mode allows for structured learning, helping you review concepts step by step while tracking your progress. This mode helps in systematic learningand ensures a step-by-step approach to mastering medical concepts.
Practice Mode
In Practice Mode, you can actively test your knowledge by selecting a specific category. This Mode will present a series of random questions from that category, each with four answer options. As you select and submit an answer, you'll receive instant feedback on whether your choice was correct or incorrect. After each submission, a detailed explanation of the correct answer is provided. Throughout the session, the app tracks your progress, displaying the number of correct and incorrect answers, helping you analyze your performance and improve your understanding.
Exam Mode
In Exam Mode, you can take a full-length test by selecting up to 200 questions from all categories. As you attempt and submit answers, you'll receive instant feedback on whether your response is correct or incorrect. Once the exam is completed, a detailed performance report is generated, showing the total number of exams completed, questions attempted, and a breakdown of correct and incorrect answers. Additionally, a category-wise performance chart helps you analyze your strengths and weaknesses, allowing you to focus on areas that need improvement. This mode provides a real exam-like experience, helping you track progress and enhance your knowledge effectively.
Bookmarks
In Bookmark Mode, you can easily access all the questions you have saved from Question Mode and Practice Mode in one place. This allows you to quickly revisit important questions for review and better retention. You can filter your bookmarked questions by categories, making it convenient to focus on specific topics or subjects. Whether you want to revise tricky questions or strengthen your weak areas, this mode provides a structured way to organize and review key concepts efficiently.
